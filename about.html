Char2tree



Char2tree project was created by students of Computational Linguisics in HSE'21 (Nikita Sykhrannov, Elizaveta Ezhergina and Karina Mischenkova) under the mentorship of Francis Tyers, PhD. The data used in model training in Chukchi is courtesy of chuklang.ru, a corpus of Amguema Chukchi dialect.
