Char2tree



Char2tree is a tool for sentence tree generation that currently operates for several languages including Amguema dialect of Chukchi (courtesy of chuklang.ru), second language, and something else. To generate a tree or obtain tagged/parsed/segmented data in ConLL-U format, go to the tree generator and enter your sentence in the provided field.

Originally, Char2tree was developed as an end-to-end segmenter/POS tagger/dependency parser for Universal Dependencies. It is created by 2nd-year Master's students of Computational Linguistics in HSE (Nikita Sykhrannov, Elizaveta Ezhergina, Karina Mischenkova) under the mentorship of Francis Tyers, PhD. The full code and data is available at the repo.
